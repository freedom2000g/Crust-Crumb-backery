Our Story

A tradition of baking excellence passed down through generations.

From Our Family to Yours

Founded in 2010, Crust & Crumb began as a small passion project in a home kitchen. What started as sharing homemade loaves with neighbors quickly blossomed into a beloved local institution.

We believe in the magic of simple ingredients: flour, water, salt, and time. Our naturally leavened breads undergo a slow fermentation process, which not only develops a complex flavor profile but makes them easier to digest.

Our Core Values

Quality Ingredients

We source local, organic flour and use no artificial preservatives.

Time Honored Technique

We respect the process and give our dough the time it needs.

Community

We bake for our neighbors and strive to be a welcoming gathering space.
